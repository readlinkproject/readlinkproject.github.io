The American Rocketry Challenge (TARC)

The American Rocketry Challenge is the world's largest rocket contest for middle and high school students. It tasks teams with designing, building, and flying model rockets that meet incredibly precise altitude and flight duration parameters.

Our Achievements

The 2025–2026 school year marked our official competitive debut in TARC. As a growing program, our team successfully managed complex structural engineering metrics, dynamic flight stability modeling, and practical field telemetry tracking to compete at a high level.
